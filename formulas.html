Geometric Formulas

Shape Volume Surface Area
Sphere \( V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3 \) \( A = 4 \pi r^2 \)
Cylinder \( V = \pi r^2 h \) \( A = 2 \pi r h + 2 \pi r^2 \)
Cone \( V = \frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h \) \( A = \pi r (r + l) \)
Cube \( V = a^3 \) \( A = 6a^2 \)
Rectangular Prism \( V = l \cdot w \cdot h \) \( A = 2(lw + lh + wh) \)
